## ACT TWO — ELLIS ISLAND

### Scene 4: [Disembarkation & initial medical check]
- **The six-second medical:** Doctors scanning for obvious illness
- **Emotional stakes:** Fear of rejection

### Scene 5: [The Great Hall — waiting]
- **Time:** Morning to afternoon
- **Crowds:** Up to 5,000 people; noise, confusion, multiple languages
- **Interpreters:** Italian interpreters helping with forms
- **Sensory:** Cold stone floor, echoing hall, children crying

### Scene 6: [The eye exam — trachoma check]
- **The buttonhook:** Most feared moment
- **Stakes:** A positive result means deportation
- **Emotional peak:** Family's terror for their children's eyes

### Scene 7: [Legal inspection — the questions]
- **Name, origin, destination, work, money**
- **Literacy test:** Can they read? (Many southern Italians were illiterate)
- **The moment of decision:** Inspector stamps card — ADMITTED or DETAINED

### Scene 8: [The ferry to Manhattan — dusk/night]
- **Time:** ~5:00 PM (after sunset at 4:52 — see astronomy notes)
- **Dark harbor:** Crescent moon low over water, ice floes, coal-smoke haze
- **First sight of Manhattan skyline:** Lit windows, dim streetlights
- **Emotional shift:** Relief mixed with new dread

## ACT THREE — THE NEW WORLD

### Scene 9: [Arrival in Little Italy — Mulberry Street]
- **Time:** Evening, dark streets
- **Sensory:** Pushcart vendors, cooking smells, Italian voices, tenement hallways
- **Welcome/exploitation:** A padrone or relative meets them

### Scene 10: [The tenement — first night]
- **Housing:** See cultural notes on tenements
- **Sensory:** Crowded room, shared toilet in hallway, thin walls, noise
- **Children:** Wonder mixed with fear

### Scene 11: [First days — work, survival, adaptation]
- **Finding work:** Padrone system, manual labor
- **Cultural friction:** Irish neighbors, language barriers, American customs
- **Small victories:** First earnings, familiar food, community connections

### Scene 12: [Closing scene — Sunday dinner]
- **Even with poverty, the Sunday pasta is made**
- **Symbol:** The waxing crescent moon seen through the tenement window — hope growing
- **Final image:** Family around a table, in a cramped room, in a strange city — together
